# Comprehensive Study on Hospital Management Systems (HMS)
Prepared by **LumenPath Trading and Services**


<div class="card">
<p>This site presents a complete overview of an HMS along with a deployable partner proposal and a detailed implementation playbook for rolling out to multiple organizations within the same country.</p>
</div>


## Objectives
- Operational efficiency via automation of scheduling, billing, and inventory.
- Unified data platform across departments.
- Improved patient care with real-time clinical access.
- Cost reduction and error minimization.
- Compliance with healthcare data protection laws.
- Scalable design for multi-facility networks.


## Core Modules
- Patient Administration, EMR, Billing/RCM, LIS, Pharmacy
- Inventory & Assets, HR/Payroll, Admin & Analytics


## Technical Architecture
- Presentation: Web/mobile portals
- Application: Modular services with workflow engine
- Data: Per‑tenant Postgres + object storage
- Integration: REST/GraphQL, HL7/FHIR, PACS/RIS
